Blue Russian Rats

We've Got The Blues!

We have a mixture of boys and girls ranging all 8 to 12 weeks old. They have a variety of markings on their heads and bellies, but all of them are Blue Russian Rats.

Russian Blue Rats are the way to go! These beautiful rats are amazing companions because they have the prettiest, softest blue/grey coat, they love to be held and played with, they are very healthy, and they are not stinky!

If you are considering a gerbil, hamster, ferret, or even a small dog, you are doing yourself a disservice by not considering a rat instead. They are more cuddly and never aggressive.

We will only sell these social animals in pairs.

More About Russian Blue Rats

If you're looking for soft and snuggly, the Russian blue rat might be just the rat for you. Their coats are often short, thick & very plush to the touch.

Temperment

Each rat has their own personality, but in general, a Blue Russians temperment can generally be described as charming and clean. No matter what, you can expect your rat to love you immensily as you show them love and attention.

Show Rats

If you want to the perfect show rat, Russian Blue Rats are a great option! Becuase of their natural slate blue grey coloring, they never look dingy or dirty. Choose one of our rats with an even color throughout their coat. No hints of brown. And absolutely no white spots (because this will get them disqualified).

Protip: You'll only need to wash their tail before a show. If you do want to give your beauties a bath, make sure to do it a week before showtime. This gives time for their natural oils to return. That magical metallic sheen will glisten!
