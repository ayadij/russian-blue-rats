Frequently Asked Questions

Should I get a boy or a girl?

Boys are more cuddly and chill. They grow to be much larger than the girls. Girls are more active and fun to train. They don't have balls.

Why do we have to buy in pairs?

Rats are very social animals. They need a companion to be healthy and happy and to live the best life!

Do the rats come with a cage?

They do not... But if you need advice on cages, we can help you find the best one for your rats.

Why does my rat have a bloody nose?

The red you see around your rat's nose is actually not blood! It's called porphyrin and acts sort of like your snot does. Usually, it is just from dry air.

How long do pet rats typically live?

Typically 2 years but often much longer if they are very healthy throughout life. We advise a healthy diet, lots of play time each day, and a humidifier if you live in a dry climate (like Utah).

What if they get sick after I buy them?

animal doctors are usually the only ones who treat rats, but feel free to talk to us if you have questions or need advice before going to the doctors. We have experience with 60 rats to date :)
